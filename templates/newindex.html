About the Project

Early Detection Saves Lives

Melanoma is the most dangerous form of skin cancer, but when detected early, the 5-year survival rate is over 99%. Our project leverages the power of Convolutional Neural Networks to analyze dermoscopic images and assist healthcare professionals in early detection.

Deep Learning Approach

Using state-of-the-art CNN architecture, our model has been trained on thousands of dermoscopic images to recognize patterns and features associated with malignant melanoma, achieving high accuracy in classification.
